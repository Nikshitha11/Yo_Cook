Dal Soup Recipe

Dal Soup

Dal soup is a simple and nutritious recipe, ideal for babies, toddlers, or anyone looking for a light, healthy meal. Made with moong dal and minimal spices, this soup is both delicious and easy to digest.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup moong dal (yellow lentils)
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • A pinch of turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ghee (optional, for toddlers)

Instructions:

  1. Wash moong dal thoroughly until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pressure cooker or pot, add the dal, water, and turmeric. Cook until the dal turns soft.
  3. Once cooked, mash the dal or blend it into a smooth consistency using a blender.
  4. Add more water if required to adjust the consistency.
  5. Season with salt and optional cumin powder for flavor.
  6. Simmer the soup for a few minutes and serve warm.
