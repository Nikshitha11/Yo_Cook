Brinjal Fry Recipe

Brinjal Fry

Brinjal Fry, also known as Vankaya Vepudu, is a South Indian side dish where brinjal (eggplant) is stir-fried with aromatic spices. It's a quick and delicious accompaniment for rice or roti.

Ingredients:

  • 250 grams brinjal (eggplant)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Wash and chop brinjal into medium-sized pieces. Immerse them in salted water to prevent discoloration.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard and cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add curry leaves and green chilies. SautÃ© for a minute.
  4. Drain the brinjal pieces and add them to the pan. Stir well.
  5. Sprinkle turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Mix to coat the brinjal evenly.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat until the brinjal is tender, stirring occasionally.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and cook for another 2 minutes.
  8. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.
