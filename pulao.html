Vegetable Pulao Recipe

Vegetable Pulao

Vegetable Pulao is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with a mix of vegetables and spices. It is a perfect one-pot meal for lunch or dinner and is best served with raita or curry.

Ingredients:

  • For Rice:
    • 1 cup basmati rice
    • 2 cups water
    • 1 tablespoon ghee or oil
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 2 cloves
    • 1 cardamom pod
  • For Vegetables:
    • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
    • 1/2 cup chopped beans
    • 1/4 cup peas
    • 1 small potato, chopped
    • 1 small onion, sliced
    • 1 tomato, chopped
  • For Spices:
    • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
    • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
    • Salt to taste
    • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Rice:
    • Wash the basmati rice thoroughly and soak it for 15-20 minutes. Drain the water.
    • In a pressure cooker, add ghee or oil, and heat it.
    • Add the bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cloves, and cardamom. Saute for a few seconds until fragrant.
    • Add the soaked rice and stir gently to coat the rice with the spices.
  2. Cook Vegetables:
    • In a separate pan, heat some oil and sautÃ© the onions until they become translucent.
    • Add the ginger-garlic paste and sautÃ© for another minute.
    • Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
    • Add the chopped vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, and potatoes) and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Combine Rice and Vegetables:
    • Add the cooked vegetables to the rice in the pressure cooker.
    • Add turmeric powder, garam masala powder, cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
    • Add 2 cups of water and bring it to a boil.
    • Close the lid of the pressure cooker and cook for 2 whistles on medium heat. Then, reduce the heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Finishing Touch:
    • Allow the pressure to release naturally. Open the lid and fluff the rice gently with a fork.
    • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
