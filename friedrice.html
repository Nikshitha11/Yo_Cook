Veg Fried Rice Recipe

Veg Fried Rice

Veg fried rice is a flavorful, quick meal made with mixed vegetables, rice, and soy sauce. It is easy to prepare and perfect for a busy day.

Ingredients:

  • For Rice:
    • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • For Stir Fry:
    • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
    • 1/2 cup chopped beans
    • 1/4 cup chopped capsicum
    • 1/4 cup peas
    • 1 tablespoon oil
    • 1/2 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
    • 1 teaspoon vinegar
    • 1/4 teaspoon pepper powder
    • 1 tablespoon chopped spring onions (optional)
    • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Vegetables:
    • Chop the vegetables and keep them aside.
  2. Fry the Vegetables:
    • Heat oil in a wok or pan, add ginger-garlic paste, and sautÃ© for a minute.
    • Add carrots, beans, capsicum, and peas. Cook on medium heat until tender but still crunchy.
  3. Combine Rice and Veggies:
    • Add the cold rice to the pan and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
    • Mix in soy sauce, vinegar, and pepper powder. Stir well until all ingredients are combined.
  4. Garnish:
    • Top with chopped spring onions if desired. Add salt to taste.
