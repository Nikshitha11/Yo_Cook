Lemon Rice Recipe

Lemon Rice

Lemon Rice is a popular South Indian dish that is both flavorful and refreshing, made with rice, tangy lemon juice, and a blend of spices. It's perfect for a quick meal and can be enjoyed on its own or with a side of yogurt or curry.

Ingredients:

  • For Rice:
    • 1 cup rice (preferably basmati or sona masuri)
    • 2 cups water
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • For Tempering:
    • 1 tablespoon oil
    • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
    • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 2-3 dried red chilies
    • 1 tablespoon peanuts (optional)
    • 1 tablespoon chopped cashews (optional)
    • 1 sprig curry leaves
    • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • Other Ingredients:
    • 2 tablespoons lemon juice (adjust to taste)
    • 1 tablespoon grated fresh coconut (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Rice:
    • Rinse the rice in water until it runs clear. Cook the rice with 2 cups of water and salt until itâ€™s soft but not mushy. You can use a pressure cooker or a pan to cook the rice. Once done, fluff the rice and let it cool slightly.
  2. Prepare Tempering:
    • In a pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
    • Add cumin seeds, dried red chilies, curry leaves, peanuts, and cashews. SautÃ© for a couple of minutes until the nuts are golden brown.
    • Next, add turmeric powder and red chili powder. Stir well and cook for another minute.
  3. Combine Rice and Spices:
    • Add the cooked rice to the pan with the tempering. Mix gently to combine the rice with the spices.
    • Stir in the lemon juice and grated coconut (if using). Adjust the salt and lemon juice to taste.
  4. Serve:
    • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with yogurt or curry on the side.
