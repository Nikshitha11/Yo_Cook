Palak Paneer Recipe

Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer is a classic Indian dish made with fresh spinach (palak) and paneer (Indian cottage cheese). It's creamy, flavorful, and pairs well with naan, roti, or rice.

Ingredients:

  • 200 grams paneer, cubed
  • 200 grams spinach (palak), washed and blanched
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 tablespoons cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Blanch the spinach: Boil water, add spinach leaves for 2-3 minutes, then transfer to cold water. Blend into a smooth puree.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sautÃ© until golden brown. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; cook until aromatic.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates. Stir in turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala.
  5. Add the spinach puree, mix well, and simmer for 3-4 minutes. Adjust salt.
  6. Add paneer cubes and gently mix. Simmer for 2-3 minutes. Add cream if using and stir.
  7. Crush and sprinkle kasuri methi. Mix well and turn off the heat.
