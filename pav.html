Pav Bhaji Recipe

Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food consisting of a spicy vegetable mash served with buttered bread rolls. It's a delicious and comforting meal that is loved by people of all ages.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup boiled green peas
  • 1/4 cup chopped capsicum
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon pav bhaji masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 pav (bread rolls)
  • Butter for toasting
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sautÃ© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Mix in the chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add capsicum, turmeric, chili powder, and pav bhaji masala. SautÃ© for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add boiled green peas and mashed potatoes. Mix well.
  6. Pour in some water and cook until the mixture reaches a thick consistency.
  7. Toast the pav with butter on a griddle until golden.
  8. Garnish the bhaji with fresh coriander leaves and a dollop of butter.
