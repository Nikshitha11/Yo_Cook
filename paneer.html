Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy dish made with soft paneer cubes in a smooth and flavorful tomato-based gravy. Itâ€™s a popular dish in Indian restaurants and is perfect when paired with naan or rice.

Ingredients:

  • For the Gravy:
    • 1 tablespoon butter
    • 1 tablespoon oil
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 2 tomatoes, pureed
    • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
    • Salt to taste
    • 1/2 cup water
    • 1/2 cup fresh cream
    • 1 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
    • Paneer cubes (200g)
    • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
  • For Garnish:
    • Coriander leaves, chopped
    • Butter (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Gravy:
    • Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add finely chopped onions and sautÃ© until golden brown.
    • Add ginger-garlic paste and sautÃ© for a minute.
    • Pour in the pureed tomatoes and cook for 5-7 minutes until the oil separates from the masala.
  2. Cook the Gravy:
    • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt to the tomato gravy. Mix well.
    • Pour in water to adjust the consistency, then add cream and kasuri methi. Stir and cook for another 5 minutes.
  3. Add the Paneer:
    • Add the paneer cubes to the gravy. Stir gently and cook for 5-7 minutes, allowing the paneer to soak up the flavors.
  4. Garnish:
    • Top with chopped coriander leaves and a dollop of butter before serving.
