Fragrant Ghee Rice

Ingredients:

  • For the Rice:
    • 1 cup basmati rice
    • 2 cups water
    • 1 tablespoon ghee
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 2-3 cloves
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 1-2 cardamom pods
    • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preparing Rice:
    • Rinse basmati rice thoroughly and soak for 30 minutes. Drain the rice before cooking.
  2. Cooking Rice:
    • Heat ghee in a pan, add the whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, and cardamom), and sautÃ© for a minute.
    • Add the soaked and drained rice, sautÃ© for 2-3 minutes to coat the rice with ghee.
    • Add water, salt, and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for 12-15 minutes.
  3. Fluff and Serve:
    • Let the rice rest for a few minutes before fluffing it with a fork. Serve hot.
