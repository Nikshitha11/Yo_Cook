Chana Masala Recipe

Chana Masala

Chana Masala is a popular North Indian curry made with chickpeas simmered in a spiced tomato gravy. It is rich, flavorful, and pairs perfectly with rice, roti, or naan.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (or 2 cups canned chickpeas)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon chana masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Soak dried chickpeas overnight. Cook in a pressure cooker until soft, or use canned chickpeas.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, and allow to splutter.
  3. SautÃ© onions until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook until aromatic.
  4. Add tomatoes, cook until soft. Add chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, coriander, cumin powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Add cooked chickpeas and 1 cup water. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Sprinkle chana masala powder and mix well. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
