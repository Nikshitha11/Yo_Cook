Akki Roti Recipe

Flavorful Akki Roti

Akki Roti is a popular Karnataka breakfast dish made with rice flour, vegetables, and spices. This savory flatbread is typically enjoyed with chutney or curry. Here's how you can prepare it at home:

Ingredients:

  • For the Dough:
    • 2 cups rice flour
    • 1/4 cup grated carrot (optional)
    • 1/4 cup chopped onions
    • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
    • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
    • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
    • Salt to taste
    • Water (as needed)
    • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a large bowl, add rice flour, chopped onions, green chilies, coriander leaves, grated carrots, cumin seeds, mustard seeds, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.
    • Gradually add water little by little and mix well until a soft, non-sticky dough forms. You may need to adjust the water quantity depending on the type of rice flour used.
    • Knead the dough for 2-3 minutes to make it smooth. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Roll the Roti:
    • Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a ball.
    • Place a ball on a flat surface and flatten it with your palms to form a round disc (about 6-7 inches in diameter). You can use a little rice flour to prevent sticking.
    • If the dough sticks, you can also use parchment paper or plastic to roll the dough between them.
  3. Cook the Akki Roti:
    • Heat a griddle or tawa on medium heat and lightly grease it with oil or ghee.
    • Carefully transfer the rolled roti onto the hot griddle.
    • Cook on medium heat for 2-3 minutes, then flip it over. Cook the other side for another 2-3 minutes.
    • Once both sides are golden brown and crisp, remove the roti from the tawa.
  4. Serve:
    • Serve the hot Akki Roti with coconut chutney, pickle, or any curry of your choice.

Enjoy!
