Ingredients:

  • For Rice:
    • 1 cup rice (preferably basmati)
    • 2 cups water
  • For Tomato Mixture:
    • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
    • 1 tablespoon oil
    • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
    • Salt to taste
    • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste (optional)
    • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • For Tempering:
    • 1 tablespoon ghee or oil
    • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1/4 teaspoon hing (asafoetida)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Rice:
    • Wash the rice thoroughly and cook it with 2 cups of water until soft. Let it cool.
  2. Cook Tomato Mixture:
    • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
    • Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and sugar. Cook until the tomatoes soften and release their juices.
    • If desired, add tamarind paste for tanginess. Stir well and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Combine Rice and Tomato Mixture:
    • Add the cooked rice to the tomato mixture and gently mix to combine.
    • Cook the rice and tomato mixture for another 5â€“7 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.
  4. Prepare Tempering:
    • In a small pan, heat ghee or oil. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and hing. Let them splutter, then pour the tempering over the rice.
  5. Garnish:
    • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Serving:
