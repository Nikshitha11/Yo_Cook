Beans Poriyal Recipe

Beans Poriyal

Beans Poriyal is a traditional South Indian stir-fry dish made with tender green beans, coconut, and simple spices. It is healthy, flavorful, and pairs perfectly with steamed rice and dal.

Ingredients:

  • 250 grams green beans, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 dried red chilies, broken
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and chop the green beans finely.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add urad dal and fry until golden brown.
  4. Add curry leaves, dried red chilies, and turmeric powder. Stir for a minute.
  5. Add the chopped beans and salt. Mix well.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat until the beans are tender, stirring occasionally.
  7. Once cooked, add grated coconut and mix well.
  8. Cook for another 2 minutes and turn off the heat.
