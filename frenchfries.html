French Fries Recipe

French Fries

French Fries are a popular snack made by deep-frying potatoes. These crispy and golden fries are an absolute favorite for many and can be paired with various dips or enjoyed as a side dish.

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon corn flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (for soaking)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the potatoes:
    • Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin strips (about 1/4 inch thick).
    • Soak the cut potatoes in water for about 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  2. Fry the potatoes:
    • After soaking, drain the water and pat the potatoes dry using a kitchen towel.
    • Mix the potatoes with corn flour, red chili powder, black pepper, and salt.
    • Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the potatoes in batches until golden brown and crispy.

Serving:
